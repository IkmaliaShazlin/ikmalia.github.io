Sports Equipment Rental System

C++ Interactive Rental Management

A C++-based rental management system where customers can rent sports equipment, update their cart, search items, and generate receipts. The program applies Stack, Queue, and Sorting algorithms to simulate a complete rental process with efficient item tracking and cost calculation.

Equipment Menu & Options

Displays all available sports equipment across different categories, from badminton and baseball to hockey and football. Customers can choose their preferred items and select from multiple menu options for managing their cart.

Add Item to Cart

Users enter the equipment ID, quantity, and number of rental days. Quantity helps organize multiple rentals, but the rental cost is calculated based solely on duration — at RM50 per day.

Edit Item in Cart

Customers can update item details such as rental days or quantity. After editing, the system automatically recalculates the total rental cost using the updated values.

Delete Item from Cart

Allows users to remove specific items from the cart instantly using their item ID, ensuring easy management of the rental list.

Display Cart (Sorted)

Displays all equipment in the cart sorted by rental cost — from lowest to highest — using the Bubble Sort algorithm. Each item includes name, quantity, days, and total rental fee.

Search Item in Cart

The Linear Search algorithm lets users find items by entering their equipment ID, making it fast to locate and check details.

Display Latest Item (Stack)

Implements a Stack structure to show the most recently added item — following the “Last In, First Out” (LIFO) concept.

Display First Item (Queue)

Uses a Queue structure to show the earliest added item in the cart, following the “First In, First Out” (FIFO) rule.

Display Receipt

Generates a detailed receipt that includes all rented items, their rental duration, and total cost. A perfect summary of the entire rental transaction.
